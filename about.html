My name is Christian Harwood born and raised in Arizona my dad was a sucessful musician from Canada and his influence on me made me who I am today.

Becasue my parent were split I lived on both sides of the valley in Mesa it was a big party city and on my moms side was where I met a more rough area but I am in no way a gangster.

My mission is to reach out to kids like me and show them that they need to accept the person that they are and be themselves. In a world where gang related music is glorified this is a safe enviornment for kids that aren't around that and even kids that are to feel like they fit in.

The story behind my name is pretty simple I was unsure about what I should call myslef and my mom came up with the name ¢-note which is a hundred dollar bill but also is a note in music which fit me because I know my worth is high just like money.

A big belief system I keep is yin yang a balance between dark and light, we need to accept our darkness before we can enjoy our light.
